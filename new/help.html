Frequently Asked Questions

Investment

How do I start investing?
To start investing, go to the "Invest Now" section from the sidebar menu. Choose your preferred investment plan (Starter, Premium, or Elite), select your payment method, and follow the instructions to complete your investment.
What are the minimum and maximum investment amounts?
The minimum investment starts at Rs 10,000 with our Starter Plan. The maximum investment depends on the plan you choose: Starter Plan (Rs 10,000), Premium Plan (Rs 50,000), and Elite Plan (Rs 100,000).

Withdrawals

How do I withdraw my earnings?
To withdraw your earnings, navigate to the "Withdraw" section in the sidebar menu. Enter the amount you wish to withdraw, select your preferred payment method, and follow the verification process. Withdrawals are typically processed within 24-48 hours.
What are the withdrawal limits?
The minimum withdrawal amount is Rs 1,000. Maximum daily withdrawal limits vary based on your account level and verification status. Standard accounts can withdraw up to Rs 50,000 per day.

Account Management

How do I change my password or PIN?
To change your password or PIN, go to "Settings" in the sidebar menu. Under the "Security Settings" section, you can update your password and PIN. Make sure to enter your current password for verification.
How do I verify my account?
Account verification requires submitting valid identification documents. Go to Settings > Profile Settings and look for the "Verify Account" section. Upload the required documents (CNIC, proof of address) and wait for approval.
